Why a Real time Dashboard?

Project Progress Monitoring


Real-time Visualization: Track construction progress instantly with live updates on project milestones, completion percentages, and critical timelines

Resource Allocation: Optimize manpower, equipment, and materials based on real-time data, ensuring efficient resource utilization and timely project delivery

Early Issue Identification: Identify potential delays or bottlenecks as they occur, allowing for immediate intervention to prevent further setbacks

Safety and Compliance Management


Incident Monitoring: Receive instant notifications for safety incidents, enabling quick response and mitigation measures to ensure a secure working environment

Compliance Tracking: Real-time tracking of regulatory compliance metrics, ensuring adherence to safety standards and minimizing the risk of penalties or legal issues

Emergency Response Planning: Use live data to enhance emergency response preparedness by identifying potential safety risks and implementing proactive safety measures

More

Cost Control and Budget Management


Budget Tracking: Monitor project expenses in real time, allowing for proactive cost management, preventing budget overruns and helps leeping a track

Resource Efficiency: Analyze data on material usage, labor costs, and equipment expenses to identify opportunities for cost-saving measures

Financial Forecasting: Use real-time financial data to make informed decisions, optimize spending, and maintain financial transparency throughout the construction project
